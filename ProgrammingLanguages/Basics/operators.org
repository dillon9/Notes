behave similar to [[id:0835ea0d-ca22-4d0c-97fb-9454087c6c78][Functions]], but differ syntacticaly. Common examples of
operators include:

- Comparison operations: (>, <, etc ...)
- Logical operations: (AND, OR, NOT, etc ...)
